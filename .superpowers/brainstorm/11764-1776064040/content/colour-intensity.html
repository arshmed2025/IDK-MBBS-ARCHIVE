How bold should the colour be?

This sets the intensity across the whole site — hero, cards, sidebar, tiles.

Subtle tints

Soft coloured backgrounds, strong colour accents/borders. White cards with vivid left-borders. Feels clean and professional — colour as highlights, not fills.

Vivid with gradients

Gradient hero banner (purple→blue), gradient card backgrounds per subject colour. Bold and energetic — feels like a modern study app.

Dark hero + full colour cards

Dark/black hero with glowing gradient accents, each subject card is solid-coloured (not white). High contrast, memorable, premium feel.
